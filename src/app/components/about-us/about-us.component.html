About-Us

Located a few meters from Aptech Training Center, the restaurant La Poma offers, for over 40 years, a gastronomic universe that you can enjoy from 8:30 to 1:00, non stop kitchen and service. From breakfast or brunch to dinner, we offer you authentic Mediterranean food, with the best tapas, pastas, pizzas, meat and fish as well as tasty Fried rice and Jollof rice. We want to be your reference restaurant in the center of Lagos, come and discover it
